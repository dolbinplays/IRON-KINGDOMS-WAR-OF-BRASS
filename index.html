New War of Brass



Solo and multiplayer: set slot 1 to Human and the rest to AI for solo skirmish, or mix Human / AI slots for Discord games. Closed slots are not used.

Weekend Multiplayer Rules

Turn handoff: take your turn, press End Turn, press Save Game, and post the JSON file to Discord. The next player downloads and loads it. Solo mode works by setting extra slots to AI.

Commander selection: choose a heroic commander in New Game. Each has a unique portrait, identity, aura emphasis, and AI flavor.

Commander aura: units within 3 tiles heal at turn start and gain class-specific attack/defense bonuses.

Observer influence: defeated players gain tokens each round and can use small but meaningful powers to keep playing.

Counter examples: Pikemen punish Cavalry, Cavalry overrun Artillery, Artillery cracks Steam Tanks, Riflemen shoot Airships, Assassins hunt Mages, Steam Tanks crush Infantry.

Terrain combat: Forests, hills, mountains, towns, cities, and fortresses reduce incoming damage. Roads and plains provide little cover.

Squads: troop units represent 10 individuals, heavy units represent 5 vehicles/sections, and commanders are heroes. Casualties are shown in zoomed battle scenes as HP falls.

Audio: press Enable Audio once if your browser blocks autoplay. This branch includes your complete imported ElevenLabs MP3 soundtrack files in /assets/audio/music, alternate takes for every major theme, a soundtrack set selector, plus the fixed single-track music controller and WebAudio SFX fallback.
